All About Alvaro Feola!


Gaming Life:

Ever since I was a little kid I was addicted to gaming on both consoles and on the PC. From my first console, the Playstation 1, to my current console the Xbox one S, I have always devoted most of my time to developing my gaming skills in order to play competitively among the gaming community. My deep interest and devotion to the gaming scene is what developed my current life goal of becoming a computer scientist and eventually into a game developer and security designer.

Whether it be learning musical score or listening to the lastest hits on the top 100 bilboards, music has always been a major influence in my life. Music allowed me to view the world in a different light. It also allowed me to better myself whether it be through improvement of grades or becoming more sociable with others around me. Music will always be a major part of my life.
